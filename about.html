About ExoPlanet AI

Advancing the frontiers of exoplanet discovery through artificial intelligence and machine learning.

Our Mission

ExoPlanet AI is dedicated to democratizing exoplanet discovery by making advanced AI-powered detection tools accessible to researchers, educators, and space enthusiasts worldwide. Our mission is to accelerate the pace of exoplanet discovery and contribute to humanity's understanding of planetary systems beyond our own.

Advance Discovery

Develop cutting-edge AI algorithms to identify exoplanets with unprecedented accuracy and efficiency.

Educate & Inspire

Provide educational resources and interactive tools to inspire the next generation of astronomers and data scientists.

Foster Collaboration

Create an open platform that enables researchers worldwide to collaborate on exoplanet detection and analysis.

NASA Space Apps Challenge 2025

This project was developed as part of the NASA Space Apps Challenge 2025, the world's largest global hackathon. We're proud to contribute to NASA's mission of space exploration and scientific discovery.
